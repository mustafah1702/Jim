Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Jim ("we", "our", "the app") is a workout tracking application. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: Your email address and password (hashed) when you create an account.
  • Workout data: Exercises, sets, reps, weights, and workout timestamps that you log in the app.
  • Preferences: Your chosen weight unit, rest timer duration, and theme preference.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the app's functionality — tracking your workouts, calculating personal records, and displaying your progress. We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides the database and authentication infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting.

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Portability: Contact us to request an export of your data.

Children's Privacy

Jim is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
